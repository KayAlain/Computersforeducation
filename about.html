OUR MISSION

Computers for Schools New Brunswick (CFSNB) is dedicated
to enhancing digital literacy and bridging the digital divide by providing refurbished technology to schools, non-profit organizations, libraries, and Indigenous communities across New Brunswick. Our mission is to ensure that every student and community member has access to the technology they need to succeed in the digital age.

OUR HISTORY

CFSNB was established as part of the national Computers for Schools program, which was launched in 1993 by Industry Canada. The initiative was created to address the growing need for technology in Canadian schools and to promote environmental sustainability by refurbishing and reusing surplus computers. Since its inception, the program has grown significantly, distributing millions of computers across the country.

WHAT WE DO

Collection and Refurbishment

CFSNB collects donated computers and related equipment from individuals, businesses, and government agencies. Each piece of equipment undergoes a thorough assessment to determine its suitability for refurbishment. Our team of skilled technicians and volunteers then refurbishes the computers, ensuring they are in good working condition and equipped with the necessary software.

Distribution

Once refurbished, the computers are distributed to eligible recipients, including K-12 schools, post-secondary institutions, non-profit organizations, libraries, and Indigenous communities throughout New Brunswick. We work closely with these organizations to understand their specific needs and provide them with technology solutions that support their educational and operational goals.

Environmental Stewardship

By refurbishing and reusing computers, CFSNB plays a vital role in reducing electronic waste and promoting environmental sustainability.
We are committed to extending the life of technology and minimizing the impact of
e-waste on our planet.
Our efforts contribute to a circular economy where resources are reused and recycled, rather than discarded.

Community Engagement

CFSNB is deeply embedded in the community, and our work is made possible by the generous support of our donors and volunteers. We actively engage with local businesses, government agencies, and community groups to raise awareness about our program and the importance of digital inclusion. Our volunteer program offers individuals the opportunity to gain valuable skills and experience while making a meaningful contribution to their community.

Impact

Our work has a profound impact on educational outcomes and community development. By providing access to technology, we help to level the playing field for students from all backgrounds, ensuring that they have the tools they need to succeed academically and in their future careers. For non-profits and community organizations, access to reliable technology enables them to operate more efficiently and effectively, enhancing their capacity to serve their communities.

Get Involved

There are many ways to support CFSNB and our mission.
Whether you are an individual looking to donate a computer, a business interested in partnering with us, or someone who wants to volunteer your time and skills, we welcome your involvement. Your contributions help us to continue our work and make a lasting difference in the lives of those we serve. Together, we can create a more connected and inclusive future for all New Brunswickers.
