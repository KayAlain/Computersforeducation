Donate Used and Old Devices

We accept donations of used and old devices, including laptops, desktops, tablets, and more. Your donated devices will be refurbished and provided to those in need.

How to Donate:
  • Ensure the device is in working condition or can be easily repaired.
  • Remove all personal data from the device before donating.
  • Package the device securely to prevent damage during transit.

You can drop off your device at our collection center or request a pick-up service.

Drop-Off Location:

1234 Donation Street, City, State, ZIP

Request a Pick-Up:

Contact us at (555) 123-4567 or email pickup@cfs.com to schedule a pick-up time.
