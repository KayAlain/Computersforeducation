WHO WE ARE

CFS-NB is a registered charity and is affiliated with the National Computer for Schools Program.
In 1993, an Advisory Committee was created with representatives from various areas of education,
Telephone Pioneers, Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada, libraries and private enterprise
to administer the cleaning, refurbishment, and distribution of surplus computer units to schools and libraries.
In 1999, the Advisory Committee of CFS-NB incorporated to become Computers for Education Technology New Brunswick Limited.

YOUTH IMPACT

We provide work experience for youth, empowering them to compete in the knowledge-based economy

COMMUNITY IMPACT

We work to meet the growing demand for computers in the classroom and in the community

SCHOOL IMPACT

We provide computers to schools and students that do not have one but need one to pursue their academic and professional dreams

Computers for Schools is a national program that refurbishes computers donated from government,
private business and individuals for use by schools, libraries,
registered not-for-profit organizations and First Nations communities.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Here are some frequently asked questions and their answers

CFSNB is a non-profit organization that refurbishes donated computers and distributes them to schools, non-profit organizations, libraries, and Indigenous communities across New Brunswick. Our goal is to provide access to technology and bridge the digital divide.

Eligible recipients include K-12 schools, post-secondary institutions, non-profit organizations, libraries, and Indigenous communities within New Brunswick.

To apply for computers, please visit our website and fill out the application form available under the "Apply Now" section. Provide all the required information, and we will get in touch with you to discuss your needs.

We accept donations of desktop computers, laptops, tablets, monitors, keyboards, mice, and other computer accessories. The equipment should be in working condition or repairable.

You can donate equipment by contacting us through our website or calling our office. We will provide you with instructions on how to drop off your donation or arrange for a pickup if needed.
